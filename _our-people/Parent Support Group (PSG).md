<h2>Join our Pei Tong Dads Community!</h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PSG/PT_Dads.png">
</div>
<p>Launched in 2025, Pei Tong Dads is a new community bringing together fathers
from our school to connect, support one another, and celebrate the vital
role we play in our children's lives and families.
<br>
<br>Whether you're looking to build friendships, share parenting experiences,
or simply be part of a supportive network of dads, we'd love to have you
join us.
<br>
<br>Ready to get involved? Sign up at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/peitongdads" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/peitongdads</a> and
we'll be in touch with more information about upcoming activities and events!</p>
