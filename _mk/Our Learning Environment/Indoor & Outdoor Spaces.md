<h1>Overview</h1>
<p>The MOE Kindergarten learning environment is designed with the child in
mind. The classroom provides space for children to interact with one another
and allows them to transit seamlessly from one activity to the next. Learning
resources are placed within easy reach of the children to encourage exploration
and discovery.</p>
<p>Besides creating inviting indoor spaces to support teaching and learning,
we have also designed outdoor spaces to excite and engage children.</p>
