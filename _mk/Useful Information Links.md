by MFS or PCF. Eligible Nursery 2 (N2) children enrolled in our partner
EYC(s) are guaranteed a place at MK@Pei Tong and can choose to enroll at
MK@Pei Tong to continue their K1 and K2 programme.</p>

<h2>MK Vaccination Requirement</h2>
<p>Children who wish to enrol in MK from 1 Apr 2020 must have received at
least one dose of measles&nbsp;vaccination and have completed three doses
(primary series) of diphtheria vaccination (1M3D).

<h2>KCARE GIRO Application</h2>
<p>Fees can be paid via GIRO. The GIRO form is used for non-DBS/POSB accounts.
Please download the form and submit the original to the General Office.
<br><a href="/files/MK@Pei%20Tong/MK%20GIRO%20APPLICATION%20FORM.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">KCare GIRO Application Form</a>

<h2>KiFAS Application</h2>
<p>KiFAS subsidies are available for families where their household income
(HHI) does not exceed $12,000 or per capita income (PCI) does not exceed
$3000. To apply for KiFAS, please submit the KF1 Application Form to the
General Office.

<h2>Authorisation Pass Application</h2>
<p>Authorisation Passes are required for Parents/Guardians/Caregivers who
frequently fetch the child from the MOE Kindergarten or KCare. Each child
is entitled to 2 Authorisation Passes. If you require more than 2 passes,
please submit another form, subject to approval.</p>
<p><a href="/files/MK@Pei%20Tong/MK%20AUTHORISATION%20PASS%201.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MK AUTHORISATION PASS 1.pdf</a>
</p>
<h2>Primary 1 Registration</h2>
<p>MK students are eligible for P1 phase 2A registration.</p>
<p>Depending on the availability of P1 vacancies during phase 2A, your child
may or may not be admitted to Pei Tong Primary School.</p>
<p>For information on Primary 1 Registration Exercise, please visit: https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/p1-registration</p>
