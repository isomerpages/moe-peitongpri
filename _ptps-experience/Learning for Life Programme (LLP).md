<h2>I’MADD: Music, Art, Drama, and Dance</h2>
<p>I’MADD is our signature Learning for Life (LLP) programme that integrates
four art forms: Music, Art, Drama, and Dance. It aims to inspire creativity
and self-expression in students, fostering a lifelong appreciation for
the arts.</p>
<p>A key highlight is the P3 Musical, where every Primary 3 student performs
on stage before an audience, showcasing their talents and building confidence.</p>
<p></p>

<h3>P3 Musical</h3>
<p>All Primary 3 students perform in the National Education (NE) musical
as part of National Day Celebration, as a culmination of all 4 art forms.
They also develop confidence on stage whilst learning more about Singapore’s
history.</p>
