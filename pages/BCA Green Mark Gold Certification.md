<p>We are proud to announce that Pei Tong Primary School has been awarded
the prestigious <strong>BCA Green Mark Gold Certification</strong> by the
Building and Construction Authority (BCA). This certification recognises
our commitment to environmental sustainability and excellence in green
building practices.</p>
<p><strong>What We've Achieved:</strong> Our school has been certified under
the BCA Green Mark for Existing Schools scheme, demonstrating our dedication
to sustainable design and operations through green technologies and environmentally-friendly
practices implemented throughout our campus.</p>
<p><strong>What This Means:</strong> This achievement reflects our school's
commitment to creating a sustainable learning environment while contributing
to Singapore's environmental goals and preparing students to be responsible
global citizens.</p>
<p>The BCA Green Mark Gold Certification underscores Pei Tong Primary School's
leadership in environmental stewardship and our commitment to providing
students with a green, healthy learning environment.</p>
<p><em>The official Green Mark certification plaque will be displayed prominently on our school premises.</em>
