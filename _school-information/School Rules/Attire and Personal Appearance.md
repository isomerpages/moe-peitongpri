<h4>Uniform &amp; Name Tag</h4>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students must wear the prescribed school uniform during school and all
school activities. Modifications to the uniform are not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On days with <strong>PHE / PAL / CCA lessons</strong>, students may wear
their PE attire for the entire day.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All students are required to have their name tags sewn onto their polo
T-shirts. Name tags can be purchased from the school bookshop.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students coming to school for activities outside school hours must be
dressed in appropriate school attire.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<h4>Hair</h4>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Hair must remain in its natural color; dyeing or highlighting is strictly
prohibited.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For girls</strong>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Hair should be kept neat, tidy, and simply styled.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hair touching the collar must be tied up or cut.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only black or dark green hair accessories are allowed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For boys</strong>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Hair should be short and neat, with the fringe above the eyebrows.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Moustaches, beards, and long sideburns are not permitted</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<h4>Jewellery and accessories</h4>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>All forms of jewellery and accessories are not allowed.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>For girls</strong>:
<br>Only one pair of small, simple ear studs (with a diameter of less than
5mm) is permitted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<h4>Fingernails</h4>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Fingernails must be kept short and clean for hygiene and safety reasons.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Nail colouring is not permitted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<h4>Footwear</h4>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students must wear black shoes and the prescribed school socks with the
school uniform during all school activities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sports shoes, if required for CCA, should only be worn during CCA time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Slippers and sandals are strictly prohibited unless a valid reason is
provided.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
